Newman Dance
The annual dance is chock full of finger food, swing dancing lads, and twirling ladies. The Newman Dance is held in town for all students to come and work off their stress with a music-filled evening. The theme changes every time, who knows what will be next! The dance is your opportunity to break out the fancy clothes and spend a night letting the crazy dance moves shine!
