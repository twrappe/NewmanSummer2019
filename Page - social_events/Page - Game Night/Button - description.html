Game Night
Are you bored? Well then come join us for some board games! We have some crazy deck building games, adventure games, or some good old Clue and 52 card pick-up. You are welcome to bring any of your own games to share and play, we love new games as much as we love new players! Sugar is often present thanks to some great student bakers as well! Bring your A-game!
